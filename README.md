# 📊 Telecom Customer Churn Prediction

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for telecom companies. Losing existing customers directly impacts revenue and business growth.

This project develops a Machine Learning model to predict whether a customer is likely to leave the telecom service based on customer demographics, account information, and service usage.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The objective is to identify customers who are at high risk of churning so that the company can take proactive actions to improve customer retention.

---

## 📂 Dataset

- Dataset: Telco Customer Churn Dataset
- Records: 7,043
- Features: 21
- Target Variable: Churn (Yes / No)

---

## 💼 Business Insights

- Month-to-month contract customers are more likely to churn.
- Customers with shorter tenure have a higher churn risk.
- High monthly charges increase the probability of churn.
- Long-term contracts improve customer retention.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Introduce customer loyalty programs.
- Offer personalized retention plans.
- Improve customer support.
- Use predictive analytics to identify high-risk customers.
- Encourage long-term contract subscriptions.

This project demonstrates how Machine Learning can help telecom companies predict customer churn, improve customer retention strategies, and support data-driven business decisions.
